Makowo [maˈkɔvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kobylin-Borzymy, within Wysokie Mazowieckie County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 22 km (14 mi) north-east of Wysokie Mazowieckie and 34 km (21 mi) west of the regional capital Białystok.
