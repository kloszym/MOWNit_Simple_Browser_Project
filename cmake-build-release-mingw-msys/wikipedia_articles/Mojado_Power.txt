Mojado Power (Spanish: El mojado remojado) is a 1981 Mexican comedy film directed by and starring Alfonso Arau. The film was submitted as the Mexican entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 54th Academy Awards, but was not accepted as a nominee.


== Cast ==
Alfonso Arau as Nato Solís
Blanca Guerra as Xochitl
Pedro Damián as Joe
Priscilla Garcia as Tina
Nono Arsu as Jacobo
Socorro Bonilla as Daisy
