Denis Alekseyevich Nikonorov (Russian: Денис Алексеевич Никоноров; born 1 May 2004) is a Russian football player who plays as a right midfielder for KDV Tomsk.


== Career ==
Nikonorov made his debut for Pari Nizhny Novgorod on 4 October 2023 in a Russian Cup game against Krasnodar.
