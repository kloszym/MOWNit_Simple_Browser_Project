Le Journal de Saône-et-Loire is a French provincial daily newspaper for readers of the Saône-et-Loire département. Its headquarters are in Chalon-sur-Saône and it is printed in Chatenoy-le-Royal. It was established on 2 July 1826 in Mâcon.
Circulation in 2020: 46,253 weekday newspapers, 47,913 on Sundays.
