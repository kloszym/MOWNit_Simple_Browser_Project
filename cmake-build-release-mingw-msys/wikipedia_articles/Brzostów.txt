Brzostów [ˈbʐɔstuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Jaraczewo, within Jarocin County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) east of Jaraczewo, 5 km (3 mi) west of Jarocin, and 60 km (37 mi) south-east of the regional capital Poznań.
