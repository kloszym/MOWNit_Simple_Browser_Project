Chrapczew [ˈxrapt͡ʂɛf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Dobra, within Turek County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north-east of Dobra, 14 km (9 mi) south-east of Turek, and 130 km (81 mi) south-east of the regional capital Poznań.
