"What Do I Get?'" is a single by punk rock band Buzzcocks and its B-side is "Oh Shit". It provided Buzzcocks with their UK chart debut, peaking at No. 37 on the UK Singles Chart.
It makes an appearance in the video game Guitar Hero: Warriors of Rock and the movie Ghost World.
