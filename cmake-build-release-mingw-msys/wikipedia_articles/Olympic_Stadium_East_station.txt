Olympic Stadium East station (simplified Chinese: 奥体东站; traditional Chinese: 奧體東站; pinyin: Àotǐ Dōng Zhàn), formerly Xiangxinglu station (向兴路站; 向興路站; Xiàngxīnglù Zhàn) during planning until 2007, is a station of Line 2 of the Nanjing Metro. It started operations on 28 May 2010 along with the rest of Line 2. On 30 September 2016 the station served a peak volume of 31,300 passengers.
