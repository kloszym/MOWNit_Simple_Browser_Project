Redlands–Esri station is a train station in Redlands, California. It is located north of the headquarters of the geographic information system company Esri (Environmental Systems Research Institute), who funded the station's construction. The station opened on October 24, 2022 and is served directly by the Arrow rail line. Metrolink's San Bernardino Line express trains to Redlands–Downtown utilize the main track, but do not stop at this station.
Arrow trains run every day between approximately between 4 a.m. and 11 p.m. Trains arrive every 30 minutes in the early morning and evening and every 60 minutes from mid-morning to mid-afternoon. On weekends, trains run between 7:30 a.m. and 10 p.m., arriving every 60 minutes, except for a few hours without trains in the morning and afternoon.
