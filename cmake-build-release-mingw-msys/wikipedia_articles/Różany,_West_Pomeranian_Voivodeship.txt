Różany [ruˈʐanɨ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Bobolice, within Koszalin County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north-west of Bobolice, 32 km (20 mi) south-east of Koszalin, and 142 km (88 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 50.
