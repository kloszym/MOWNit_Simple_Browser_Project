Krogulcza Sucha [krɔˈɡult͡ʂa ˈsuxa] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Orońsko, within Szydłowiec County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 2 km (1 mi) east of Orońsko, 14 km (9 mi) north-east of Szydłowiec, and 100 km (62 mi) south of Warsaw.
