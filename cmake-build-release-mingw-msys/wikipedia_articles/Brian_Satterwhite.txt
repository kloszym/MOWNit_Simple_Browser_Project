Brian Satterwhite (born July 30, 1974) is an American composer and conductor renowned for his contributions to film music. Based in Austin, Texas, he has composed scores for over 160 short and feature films, including The Next Kill (2018), Disney's The Lone Ranger (2013), and Sushi: The Global Catch (2012).


== Early life and education ==
Born in Beeville, Texas, Satterwhite pursued his passion for music at the Berklee College of Music in Boston, Massachusetts, earning a Bachelor of Music with dual majors in Film Scoring and Composition.
