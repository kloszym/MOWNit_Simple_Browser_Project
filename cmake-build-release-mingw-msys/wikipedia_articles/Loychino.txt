Loychino (Russian: Лойчино) is a rural locality (a village) in Almozerskoye Rural Settlement, Vytegorsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. The population was 6 as of 2002.


== Geography ==
Loychino is located 69 km southeast of Vytegra (the district's administrative centre) by road. Rogozino is the nearest rural locality.
