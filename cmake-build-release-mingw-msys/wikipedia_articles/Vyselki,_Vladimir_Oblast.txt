Vyselki (Russian: Выселки) is a rural locality (a village) in Bogolyubovskoye Rural Settlement, Suzdalsky District, Vladimir Oblast, Russia. The population was 26 as of 2010. There are 2 streets.


== Geography ==
Vyselki is located 45 km southeast of Suzdal (the district's administrative centre) by road. Dorzhevo is the nearest rural locality.
