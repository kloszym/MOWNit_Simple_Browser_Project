Stradzew [ˈstrad͡zɛf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Bedlno, within Kutno County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south of Bedlno, 17 km (11 mi) south-east of Kutno, and 45 km (28 mi) north of the regional capital Łódź.
