Żukowo [ʐuˈkɔvɔ] (formerly German Suckow) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Sławno, within Sławno County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 12 kilometres (7 mi) south-east of Sławno and 175 km (109 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 340.
