Osatovo-Ramenye (Russian: Осатово-Раменье) is a rural locality (a village) in Kichmegnskoye Rural Settlement, Kichmengsko-Gorodetsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. The population was 45 as of 2002.


== Geography ==
Osatovo-Ramenye is located 40 km northeast of Kichmengsky Gorodok (the district's administrative centre) by road. Selishche is the nearest rural locality.
