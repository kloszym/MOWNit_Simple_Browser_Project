Zagórki [zaˈɡurki] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Pęczniew, within Poddębice County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south-west of Pęczniew, 25 km (16 mi) south-west of Poddębice, and 54 km (34 mi) west of the regional capital Łódź.
