Brunei competed at the 32nd SEA Games which was held from 5 to 17 May 2023 in Phnom Penh, Cambodia. Brunei delegation to the 2023 Southeast Asian Games composed of 113 athletes, officials, and coaches, the smallest contingent among all participant nations.
