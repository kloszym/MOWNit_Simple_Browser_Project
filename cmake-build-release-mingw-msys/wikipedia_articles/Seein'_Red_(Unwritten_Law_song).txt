"Seein' Red" is a song by the San Diego–based rock band Unwritten Law, released as the second single on January 14, 2002 from the band's fourth studio album, Elva, which was released on January 29 of that same year. It was written by singer Scott Russo and produced by Michael "Miguel" Happoldt. It became the highest-charting single of the band's career, holding the number one spot on Billboard's Modern Rock Tracks chart for four weeks between May and June 2002.
