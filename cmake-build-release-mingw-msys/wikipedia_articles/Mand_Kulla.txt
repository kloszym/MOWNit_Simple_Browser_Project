Mand Kulla  is a village in Bhulath Tehsil in Kapurthala district of Punjab State, India. It is located 13 kilometres (8.1 mi) from Bhulath, 32 kilometres (20 mi) away from district headquarter Kapurthala.  The village is administrated by a Sarpanch who is an elected representative of village as per the constitution of India and Panchayati raj (India).
