Chudenín (German: Chudiwa) is a municipality and village in Klatovy District in the Plzeň Region of the Czech Republic. It has about 700 inhabitants.
Chudenín lies approximately 18 kilometres (11 mi) south-west of Klatovy, 54 km (34 mi) south of Plzeň, and 130 km (81 mi) south-west of Prague.
