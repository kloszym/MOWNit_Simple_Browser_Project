Miyabe (written: 宮部) is a Japanese surname. Notable people with the surname include:

Airi Miyabe (宮部 藍梨, born 1998), Japanese volleyball player
Kingo Miyabe Japanese botanist
Miyabe Keijun (宮部 継潤, 1528–1599), Japanese Tendai monk
Miyuki Miyabe (宮部 みゆき, born 1960), Japanese writer
Miyabe Nagafusa (宮部 長房, 1581–1634), Japanese samurai
Shizue Miyabe (宮部 シズエ, born 1938), Japanese swimmer
Yasunori Miyabe (宮部 保範, born 1966), Japanese speed skater
Yukinori Miyabe (宮部 行範, 1968–2017), Japanese speed skater
