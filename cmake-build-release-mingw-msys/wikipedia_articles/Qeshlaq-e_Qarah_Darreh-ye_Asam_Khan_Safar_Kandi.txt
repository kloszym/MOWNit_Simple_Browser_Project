Qeshlaq-e Qarah Darreh-ye Asam Khan Safar Kandi (Persian: قشلاق قره دره اسمخان صفركندي) is a village in Qeshlaq-e Sharqi Rural District of Qeshlaq Dasht District in Bileh Savar County, Ardabil province, Iran.


== Demographics ==


=== Population ===
At the time of the 2006 National Census, the village's population was 41 in nine households. The following census in 2011 counted a population below the reporting threshold. The 2016 census measured the population of the village as zero.
