Třebušín is a municipality and village in Litoměřice District in the Ústí nad Labem Region of the Czech Republic. It has about 600 inhabitants.
Třebušín lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) north-east of Litoměřice, 14 km (9 mi) south-east of Ústí nad Labem, and 59 km (37 mi) north of Prague.
