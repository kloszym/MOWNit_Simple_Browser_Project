Podgórki [pɔdˈɡurki] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Świerzawa, within Złotoryja County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. It lies approximately 8 km (5 mi) south-west of Świerzawa, 21 km (13 mi) south of Złotoryja, and 86 km (53 mi) west of the regional capital Wrocław.
