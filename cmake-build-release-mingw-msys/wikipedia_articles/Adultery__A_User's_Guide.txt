Adultery: A User's Guide (French: Adultère, mode d'emploi) is a 1995 Swiss romantic comedy-drama film directed by Christine Pascal. The film was selected as the Swiss entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 68th Academy Awards, but was not accepted as a nominee.
