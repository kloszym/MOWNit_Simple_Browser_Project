Kyoya Honda (本田 響矢, Honda Kyōya, born June 20, 1999) is a Japanese actor and model. As a model, he  gained recognition after being named as the "Top 1 Handsome Senior Student" in Japan in 2016.


== Personal life ==
Honda's hobbies include playing the drums and karaoke, in addition he is skilled in kendo.
