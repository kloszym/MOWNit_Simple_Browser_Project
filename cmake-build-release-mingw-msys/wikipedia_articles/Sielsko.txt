Sielsko [ˈɕɛlskɔ] (German: Silligsdorf) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Węgorzyno, within Łobez County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland.
It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) north-west of Węgorzyno, 12 km (7 mi) south-west of Łobez, and 62 km (39 mi) east of the regional capital Szczecin.
