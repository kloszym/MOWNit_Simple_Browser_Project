Gościeszyn (Polish pronunciation: [ɡɔɕˈt͡ɕɛʂɨn]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Rogowo, within Żnin County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) south-east of Rogowo, 21 km (13 mi) south of Żnin, and 54 km (34 mi) south of Bydgoszcz.
