Tazehabad-e Amin (Persian: تازه آباد امين, also Romanized as Tāzehābād-e Amīn; also known as Majīd Khān, Tāzehābād-e Majīdkhānī, and Tāzehābād-e Moḩammad-e Amīn) is a village in Saral Rural District, Saral District, Divandarreh County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 255, in 50 families. The village is populated by Kurds.
