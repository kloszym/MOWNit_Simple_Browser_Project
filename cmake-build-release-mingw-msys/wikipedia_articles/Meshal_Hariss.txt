Meshal Hariss (Arabic: مشعل هريس, born 22 November 1996) is a Saudi Arabian professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Pro League side Al-Batin.


== Career ==
Meshal Hariss began his career at the youth teams of Al-Batin. He was first called up to the first team during the 2019–20 season. On 6 July 2022, Hariss renewed his contract with Al-Batin until 2024. On 31 August 2022, Hariss made his first team debut by coming off the bench in the 5–0 defeat to Al-Fateh. He made his first start for the club on 9 September 2022 in the 3–0 defeat to Al-Ettifaq.
