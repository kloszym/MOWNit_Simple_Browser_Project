Arisa Komiya (小宮 有紗, Komiya Arisa, born February 5, 1994) is a Japanese actress and voice actress from Shimotsuke, Tochigi. Komiya is known for starring as Yoko Usami/Yellow Buster in the 2012 Super Sentai series Tokumei Sentai Go-Busters and Dia Kurosawa in the multimedia project Love Live! Sunshine!!. She is currently affiliated with Box Corporation.
