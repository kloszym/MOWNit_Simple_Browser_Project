Adjunct Audio is an electronic music record label based in Los Angeles, California. It was founded in 2005 by Kenneth James Gibson AKA  [a]pendics.shuffle and Konstantin Gabbro and is distributed by Kompakt. Adjunct releases a range of styles from deep house and minimal techno to dub and ambient.
