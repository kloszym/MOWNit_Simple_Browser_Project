Minillas is a barrio in the municipality of Bayamón, Puerto Rico. Its population in 2010 was 39,540.


== History ==
Minillas was in Spain's gazetteers until Puerto Rico was ceded by Spain in the aftermath of the Spanish–American War under the terms of the Treaty of Paris of 1898 and became an unincorporated territory of the United States. In 1899, the United States Department of War conducted a census of Puerto Rico finding that the population of Minillas barrio was 1000.
