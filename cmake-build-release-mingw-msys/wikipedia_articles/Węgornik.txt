Węgornik [vɛnˈɡɔrnik] (German: Aalgraben) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Police, within Police County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland, close to the German border.
It lies approximately 12 kilometres (7 mi) west of Police and 20 km (12 mi) north-west of the regional capital Szczecin.
