Castelnuovo di Ceva is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Cuneo in the Italian region Piedmont, located about 90 kilometres (56 mi) southeast of Turin and about 45 kilometres (28 mi) east of Cuneo.
Castelnuovo di Ceva borders the following municipalities: Montezemolo, Murialdo, Priero, and Roccavignale.
