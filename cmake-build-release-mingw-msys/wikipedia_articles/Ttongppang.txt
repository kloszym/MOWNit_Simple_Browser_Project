Ttongppang (Korean: 똥빵; lit. 'poo bread'), or ddongbbang, is a novelty Korean street food. It is bread filled with red bean paste and walnut bits that is shaped like stylized human feces. It originated in Insa-dong, Seoul, South Korea.
