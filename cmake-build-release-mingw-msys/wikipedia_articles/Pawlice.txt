Pawlice [paˈvlit͡sɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kwidzyn, within Kwidzyn County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) east of Kwidzyn and 78 km (48 mi) south of the regional capital Gdańsk.
For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
