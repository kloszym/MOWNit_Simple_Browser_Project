He was elected to the Tamil Nadu legislative assembly as an Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam candidate from Nilakottai constituency in 1977, and 1984 elections and from Samayanallur constituency in 1980 election.


== Early life and background ==

Baluchamy was born on 25 September 1941 to Alagumalai and Vellaiammal. He pursued a Pre-University Course (PUC) before engaging in farming. His early years were marked by a strong sense of discipline, which led him to enlist in the Indian Army, where he served as a wireless operator for nearly five years during the 1960s. During his tenure in the military, he participated in several strategic operations that contributed to national security. Following his military service, he returned to Tamil Nadu and actively engaged in public service, advocating for the welfare of farmers and rural communities.
