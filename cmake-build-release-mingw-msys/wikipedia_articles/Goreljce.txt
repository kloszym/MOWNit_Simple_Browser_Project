Goreljce (pronounced [ɡɔˈɾeːltsɛ]; in older sources also Gorelice) is a small dispersed settlement in the Municipality of Radeče in eastern Slovenia. It lies in the hills south of Radeče in the historical region of Lower Carniola. The municipality is now included in the Lower Sava Statistical Region; until January 2014 it was part of the Savinja Statistical Region.
