Bracharoa is a genus of moths in the subfamily Lymantriinae. The genus was erected by George Hampson in 1905.
Species include: 

Bracharoa bistigmigera
Bracharoa charax
Bracharoa dregei
Bracharoa impunctata
Bracharoa paupera
Bracharoa quadripunctata
Bracharoa ragazzii
Bracharoa reducta
