Lentz House (Hotel Sheller) is a historic hotel located at North Manchester, Wabash County, Indiana, USA. It was built in 1881 and is a 2+1⁄2-story, rectangular, Second Empire style brick building. The third story was added in 1896 and attached to the main building is a two-story frame wing built in 1847. It has a mansard roof with dormers and a wraparound porch.: 2 
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1982.
