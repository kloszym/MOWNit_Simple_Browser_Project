Budziszów Mały [buˈd͡ʑiʂuf ˈmawɨ] (German: Klein Baudiß) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wądroże Wielkie, within Jawor County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. It lies approximately 10 km (6 mi) east of Wądroże Wielkie, 21 km (13 mi) east of Jawor, and 41 km (25 mi) west of the regional capital Wrocław.
Before 1945, it was located in Germany.
