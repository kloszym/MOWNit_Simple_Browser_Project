Stryjów, originally a village near Kraków (Poland), is a part of Kraków's Nowa Huta district since 1951.
According to the Council of Ministers regulation of December 14, 1950, the former of Mogiła municipality was abolished, and its territory was incorporated into Kraków. As a result, on January 1, 1951, the Stryjów village, located within the area of the former municipality, was also annexed to the city.
