The Island Eye News is a newspaper that serves several islands around the Charleston area, including Sullivan's Island, the Isle of Palms, Dewees Island, and Goat Island. The newspaper started in May 2005.
The paper had an average circulation of 5,311 in 2008.
It had two websites in 2008.
It had ten employees in 2008 and it came out every other Friday.
