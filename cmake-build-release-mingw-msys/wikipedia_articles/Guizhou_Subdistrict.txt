Guizhou Subdistrict (simplified Chinese: 归州街道; traditional Chinese: 歸州街道; pinyin: Guīzhōu Jiēdào) is a subdistrict in Gaizhou, Liaoning, China. As of 2020, it has 12 villages under its administration:

Guizhou Village
Huaishufang Village (槐树房村)
Baishawan Village (白沙湾村)
Yangshan Village (仰山村)
Tuanpu Village (团朴村)
Donggou Village (东沟村)
Santaizi Village (三台子村)
Longbozi Village (龙脖子村)
Xi'ertaizi Village (西二台子村)
Fangshen Village (房身村)
Pozi Village (坡子村)
Xianglanqi Village (厢兰旗村)
