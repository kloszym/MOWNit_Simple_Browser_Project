Delhi High School is a public high school in Delhi, California. It is located in Merced County. It has been established since 1998. It is part of Delhi Unified School District, which is a K-12 rural school district along with these schools: Schendel Elementary, Harmony Elementary, El Capitain Elementary, and Delhi Middle School. It has been active with the program Future Farmers of America (FFA) since 2016, and has been invited to multiple National Events.
