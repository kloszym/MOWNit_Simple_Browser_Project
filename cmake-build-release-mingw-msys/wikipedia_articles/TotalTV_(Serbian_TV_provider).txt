Total TV is a satellite television provider co-owned by Serbian Telekom Srbija. It broadcasts via Eutelsat 16A satellite (16.0E) and has over 1 million subscribers in Southeast Europe, namely Bosnia Herzegovina, Croatia, Montenegro, North Macedonia, Serbia and Slovenia.
The Croatian stake was sold to new owners of V-Investment Holdings B.V; despite the change in ownership the name of the service has remained the same. They are direct competitors to MAXtv of Hrvatski Telekom and A1.
