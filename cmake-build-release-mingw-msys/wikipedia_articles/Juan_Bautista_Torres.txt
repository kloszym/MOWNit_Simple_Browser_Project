Juan Bautista Torres (born 2 April 2002) is an Argentine tennis player.
He has a career high ATP singles ranking of world No. 226 achieved on 1 August 2022. He also has a career high doubles ranking of No. 262 achieved on 3 October 2022.


== Career ==
Torres won his maiden ATP Challenger doubles title at the 2021 Challenger de Buenos Aires with Luciano Darderi. He won his second Challenger singles title at the inaugural 2024 Dobrich Challenger edition.
