Qeshlaq-e Moranlu (Persian: قشلاق مرانلو, also Romanized as Qeshlāq-e Morānlū and Qeshlāq-e Mūrānlū; also known as Nūralū) is a village in Zahray-ye Bala Rural District, in the Central District of Buin Zahra County, Qazvin Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 569, in 130 families.
