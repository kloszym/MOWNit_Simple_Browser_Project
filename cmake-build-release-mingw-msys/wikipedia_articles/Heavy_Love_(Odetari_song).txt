"Heavy Love" (stylized in all caps) is a song by American rapper and singer Odetari. It was released on February 5, 2025, through Artist Partner Group. Produced by Rio Leyva and W4ddles, the song peaked at number nine on the US Billboard Hot Dance/Electronic Songs chart.
