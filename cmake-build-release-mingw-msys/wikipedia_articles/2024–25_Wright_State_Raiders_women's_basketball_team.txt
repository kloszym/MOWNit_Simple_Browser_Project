The 2024–25 Wright State Raiders women's basketball team represents Wright State University during the 2024–25 NCAA Division I women's basketball season. The Raiders, led by fourth-year head coach Kari Hoffman, play their home games at the Nutter Center in Fairborn, Ohio as members of the Horizon League.


== Previous season ==
The Raiders finished the 2023–24 season 18–15, 11–9 in Horizon League play, to finish in fourth place. They would defeat Milwaukee, before falling to top-seeded Cleveland State in the semifinals of the Horizon League tournament.
