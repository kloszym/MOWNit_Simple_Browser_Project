James Everette Crowell (born May 14, 1974) is an American former professional baseball pitcher, who has played in Major League Baseball (MLB) for the Cincinnati Reds (1997), Philadelphia Phillies (2004), and Florida Marlins (2005).
Crowell attended Valparaiso High School and the University of Indianapolis. He played in the Toronto Blue Jays organization in 2007.
