Hannivka (Ukrainian: Ганнівка) is a rural settlement in Kadiivka urban hromada, Alchevsk Raion (district), Luhansk Oblast (region), Ukraine. Population: 241 (2022 estimate) 


== Demographics ==
Native language distribution as of the Ukrainian Census of 2001:

Ukrainian: 89.42%
Russian: 9.19%
Others 0.28%
