Lemont Township is one of 29 townships in Cook County, Illinois, USA.  As of the 2020 census, its population was 22,645 and it contained 8,580 housing units.


== Geography ==
According to the 2021 census gazetteer files, Lemont Township has a total area of 21.02 square miles (54.44 km2), of which 20.37 square miles (52.76 km2) (or 96.90%) is land and 0.65 square miles (1.68 km2) (or 3.10%) is water.
