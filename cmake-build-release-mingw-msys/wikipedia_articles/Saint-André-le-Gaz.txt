Saint-André-le-Gaz (French pronunciation: [sɛ̃.t‿ɑ̃dʁe lə ɡaz]) is a commune in the Isère department in southeastern France.


== Geography ==
The Bourbre forms the commune's eastern border. Saint-André-le-Gaz station has rail connections to Lyon, Grenoble and Chambéry.
