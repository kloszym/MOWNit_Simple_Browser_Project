Majdan Siostrzytowski (Polish pronunciation: [ˈmajdan ɕɔstʂɨˈtɔfskʲi]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Trawniki, within Świdnik County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 23 km (14 mi) east of Świdnik and 33 km (21 mi) east of the regional capital Lublin.
