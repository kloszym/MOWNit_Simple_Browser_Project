Posejny [pɔˈsɛi̯nɨ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Sejny, within Sejny County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland, close to the border with Lithuania. It lies approximately 2 km (1 mi) south of Sejny and 110 km (68 mi) north of the regional capital Białystok.
