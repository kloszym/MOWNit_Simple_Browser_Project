Krępa [ˈkrɛmpa] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Tuliszków, within Turek County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) west of Tuliszków, 19 km (12 mi) north-west of Turek, and 98 km (61 mi) east of the regional capital Poznań.
