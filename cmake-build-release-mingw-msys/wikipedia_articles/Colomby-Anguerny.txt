Colomby-Anguerny (French pronunciation: [kɔlɔ̃bi ɑ̃ɡɛʁni]) is a commune in the department of Calvados, northwestern France. The municipality was established on 1 January 2016 by merger of the former communes of Colomby-sur-Thaon and Anguerny.
