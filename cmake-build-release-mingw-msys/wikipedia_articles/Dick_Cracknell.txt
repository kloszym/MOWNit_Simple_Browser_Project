Richard Cracknell (1929 – 2016) was an English former professional rugby league footballer who played in the 1950s. He played at representative level for Great Britain and England, and at club level for Huddersfield and Oldham, as a wing.


== Background ==
Dick Cracknell's birth was registered in Huddersfield district, West Riding of Yorkshire, England.


== Playing career ==


=== Huddersfield ===
Cracknell played on the wing in Huddersfield's 2–20 defeat by Wigan in the Championship Final during the 1949–50 season at Maine Road, Manchester on Saturday 13 May 1950.
