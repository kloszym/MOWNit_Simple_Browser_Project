Saint-Jean-de-Marsacq (French pronunciation: [sɛ̃ ʒɑ̃ də maʁsak]; Gascon: Sent Joan de Marsac or, according to the etymology, Sent Joan de Marçac) is a commune in the Landes department in Nouvelle-Aquitaine in southwestern France.
