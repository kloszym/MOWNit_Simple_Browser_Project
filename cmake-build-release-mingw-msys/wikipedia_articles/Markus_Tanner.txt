Markus Tanner (born 15 January 1954) is a Swiss former footballer who played as a striker.
He began his career with FC Basel during the 1970s, and later signed for FC Lucerne in 1981, where he played until 1985. He was also a Swiss internationalist with ten caps and one goal.
