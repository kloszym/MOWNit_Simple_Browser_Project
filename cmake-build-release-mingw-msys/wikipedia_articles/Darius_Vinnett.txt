Darius Vinnett (born September 30, 1984) is a former American football cornerback. He was signed by the St. Louis Rams as an undrafted free agent in 2007. He played college football at Arkansas and high school football at Destrehan High School.
Vinnett was also a member of the Atlanta Falcons, Florida Tuskers and Virginia Destroyers.
