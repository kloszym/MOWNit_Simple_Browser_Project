Nizhnyaya Petrovka (Russian: Нижняя Петровка) is a rural locality (a settlement) in Rasskazikhinsky Selsoviet, Pervomaysky District, Altai Krai, Russia. The population was 188 as of 2013. There are 5 streets.


== Geography ==
Nizhnyaya Petrovka is located 67 km south of Novoaltaysk (the district's administrative centre) by road. Malaya Rechka is the nearest rural locality.
