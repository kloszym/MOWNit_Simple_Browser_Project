Rogów [ˈrɔɡuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kozłów, within Miechów County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south-east of Kozłów, 11 km (7 mi) north of Miechów, and 45 km (28 mi) north of the regional capital Kraków.
