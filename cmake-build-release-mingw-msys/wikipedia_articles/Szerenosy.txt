Szerenosy [ʂɛrɛˈnɔsɨ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Juchnowiec Kościelny, within Białystok County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 3 km (2 mi) west of Juchnowiec Kościelny and 12 km (7 mi) south of the regional capital Białystok.
