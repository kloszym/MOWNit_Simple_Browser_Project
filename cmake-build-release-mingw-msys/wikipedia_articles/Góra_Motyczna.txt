Góra Motyczna [ˈɡura mɔˈtɨt͡ʂna] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Żyraków, within Dębica County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) north-west of Żyraków, 7 km (4 mi) north-west of Dębica, and 46 km (29 mi) west of the regional capital Rzeszów.
