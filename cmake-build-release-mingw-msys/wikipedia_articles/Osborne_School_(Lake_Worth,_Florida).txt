The Osborne School was a racially-segregated public school for African-American children on 1726 Douglas Street in Lake Worth Beach, Florida.


== History ==
The building was constructed in 1948 and used as a school until 1971. In 1971, Osborne was the last school in Florida to be integrated after the United States Supreme Court ordered the end of segregation in 1969. 


=== Repurposed ===
In 1980 the building was repurposed as a community education center. The building remains at 1718 S. Douglas ST, Lake Worth, FL.
