Meeting the Masters (simplified Chinese: 会见大师; traditional Chinese: 會見大師; pinyin: Huìjiàn dàshī) is collection of  45 short stories by Nobel Prize in Literature winning author Mo Yan. It was split into two parts with the second half being subtitled "Dating with the Master" (Chinese: 与大师约会; pinyin: Yǔ dàshī yuēhuì ).
Stories include:

"Fire Burning Flower Basket"
"Moonlight"
"Big Mouth"
"Lover's Lover"
"Fiction 9"
