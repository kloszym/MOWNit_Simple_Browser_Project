Nowy Dwór [ˈnɔvɨ ˈdvur] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Dąbrowa Chełmińska, within Bydgoszcz County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland. It lies 7 kilometres (4 mi) south-west of Dąbrowa Chełmińska, 15 km (9 mi) east of Bydgoszcz, and 30 km (19 mi) north-west of Toruń.
