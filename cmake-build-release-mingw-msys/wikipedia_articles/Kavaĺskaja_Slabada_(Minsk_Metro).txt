Kavaĺskaja Slabada (Belarusian: Кавальская Слабада; Russian: Ковальская Слобода) is a Minsk Metro station. It is located at the intersection of Žukoŭskaha and Varanianskaha streets. The station was opened 6 November, 2020 as the southern terminus of the line.
On 30 December 2024 a three-station extension of the line from Kavalskaya Slabada to Slutski Hastsinets was opened, and Kavaĺskaja Slabada ceased to be the terminus of the line.
