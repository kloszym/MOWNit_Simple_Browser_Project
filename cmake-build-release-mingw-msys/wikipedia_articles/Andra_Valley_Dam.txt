Andra Valley Dam, is an earthfill dam on Andra river near Maval, Pune district in the state of Maharashtra in India.


== Specifications ==
The height of the dam above lowest foundation is 40.45 m (132.7 ft) while the length is 330 m (1,080 ft). The volume content is 207.86 km3 (49.87 cu mi), and the gross storage capacity is 83,310.00 km3 (19,987.13 cu mi).
