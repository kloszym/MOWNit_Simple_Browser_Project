Dzierdziówka [d͡ʑɛrˈd͡ʑufka] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Zaleszany, within Stalowa Wola County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) east of Zaleszany, 11 km (7 mi) north-west of Stalowa Wola, and 69 km (43 mi) north of the regional capital Rzeszów.
