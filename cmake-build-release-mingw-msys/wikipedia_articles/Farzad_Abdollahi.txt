Farzad Abdollahi (Persian: فرزاد عبداللهی, born 27 October 1990 in Mianeh, Iran) is an Iranian taekwondo practitioner. He won the gold medal in the welterweight division (80 kg) at the 2011 World Taekwondo Championships in Gyeongju, South Korea.
Abdollahi is a cousin of another world taekwondo champion, Yousef Karami.
