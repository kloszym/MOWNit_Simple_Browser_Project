The Slovenian Republic Football Cup (Slovene: Slovenski republiški nogometni pokal) was the football knockout competition in SR Slovenia within the Yugoslav football system, and served as a qualifier for the main stage of the Yugoslav Cup. After the independence of Slovenia in 1991, it was transformed into the Slovenian Football Cup.
