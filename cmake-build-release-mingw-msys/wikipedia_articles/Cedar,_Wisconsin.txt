Cedar is an unincorporated community located in the town of Gurney, Iron County, Wisconsin, United States. Cedar is located on U.S. Route 2 4 miles (6.4 km) west-northwest of Saxon.


== History ==
A post office called Cedar was established in 1891, and remained in operation until it was discontinued in 1955. The community was named from groves of cedar near the town site.
