Loginovo (Russian: Логиново) is a rural locality (a village) in Biryakovskoye Rural Settlement, Sokolsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. The population was 1 as of 2002.


== Geography ==
Loginovo is located 105 km northeast of Sokol (the district's administrative centre) by road. Votchino is the nearest rural locality.
