Simone Brisighella is an Italian rugby union player.
His usual position is as a Fly-half or Fullback and he currently plays for Zebre Parma in United Rugby Championship and,and on loan, for Viadana in Italian Serie A Elite as Academy Player.
Brisighella signed for Zebre Parma in March 2023 ahead of the 2023–24 United Rugby Championship as Academy Player. He made his debut in Round 10 of the 2024–25 season against the Ulster.
In 2023 and 2024 he was named in Italy U20s squad. for annual Six Nations Under 20s Championship.
On 3 December 2024 he was called in Italy Under 23 squad for test series against Emerging Scotland.
