Zachowice [zaxɔˈvit͡sɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kąty Wrocławskie, within Wrocław County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. It lies approximately 6 km (4 mi) south of Kąty Wrocławskie, and 24 km (15 mi) south-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
