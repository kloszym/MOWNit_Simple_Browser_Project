Saint-Laurent-sur-Gorre (French pronunciation: [sɛ̃ loʁɑ̃ syʁ ɡɔʁ]; Occitan: Sent Laurenç de Gòra) is a commune in the Haute-Vienne department in the Nouvelle-Aquitaine region in west-central France.
Inhabitants are known as Laurentais.
