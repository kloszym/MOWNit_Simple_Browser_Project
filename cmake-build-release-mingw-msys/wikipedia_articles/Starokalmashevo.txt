Starokalmashevo (Russian: Старокалмашево; Bashkir: Иҫке Ҡалмаш, İśke Qalmaş) is a rural locality (a selo) and the administrative centre of Starokalmashevsky Selsoviet, Chekmagushevsky District, Bashkortostan, Russia. The population was 1,849 as of 2010. There are 19 streets.


== Geography ==
Starokalmashevo is located 15 km east of Chekmagush (the district's administrative centre) by road. Bulgar is the nearest rural locality.
