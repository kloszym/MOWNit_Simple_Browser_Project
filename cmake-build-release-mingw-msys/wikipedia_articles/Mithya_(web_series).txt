Mithya is an Indian Hindi-language, psychological thriller drama web series streaming on ZEE5. It is directed by Rohan Sippy and produced by Applause Entertainment and Vipin Agnihotri Films. The series, which is originally based on Web series Cheat 2019, was released on 18 February 2022 and stars  Huma Qureshi, Avantika Dassani, Parambrata Chatterjee, Rajit Kapur and Samir Soni in pivotal roles. The series is adapted from the British television show Cheat! Avantika Dassani made her acting debut with this series. The drama was exclusively shot in St. Paul's School, Darjeeling. Following the conclusion of the first season, a second season, titled Mithya: The Darker Chapter, was announced in October 2024 and premiered on 1 November 2024. Actor Naveen Kasturia joined the cast in a pivotal role.
