Piotry [ˈpjɔtrɨ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Miedzichowo, within Nowy Tomyśl County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 21 kilometres (13 mi) north-west of Nowy Tomyśl and 67 km (42 mi) west of the regional capital Poznań.
