Sudół [ˈsuduu̯] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Jędrzejów, within Jędrzejów County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north-west of Jędrzejów and 36 km (22 mi) south-west of the regional capital Kielce.
