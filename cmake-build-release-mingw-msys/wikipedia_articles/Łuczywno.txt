Łuczywno [wuˈt͡ʂɨvnɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Osiek Mały, within Koło County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) west of Osiek Mały, 13 km (8 mi) north-west of Koło, and 109 km (68 mi) east of the regional capital Poznań.
