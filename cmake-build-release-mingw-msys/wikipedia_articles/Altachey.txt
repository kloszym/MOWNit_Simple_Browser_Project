Altachey (Russian: Алтачей; Buryat: Алташа, Altasha) is a rural locality (an ulus) in Bichursky District, Republic of Buryatia, Russia. The population was 76 as of 2010. There is 1 street.


== Geography ==
Altachey is located 17 km north of Bichura (the district's administrative centre) by road. Petropavlovka is the nearest rural locality.
