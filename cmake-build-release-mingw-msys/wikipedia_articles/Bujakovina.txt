Bujakovina (Serbian Cyrillic: Бујаковина) is a village in the municipalities of Foča, Republika Srpska and Foča-Ustikolina, Bosnia and Herzegovina.


== Demographics ==
According to the 2013 census, its population was 36, all Bosniaks living in the Republika Srpska part, thus none living in the Foča-Ustikolina part.
