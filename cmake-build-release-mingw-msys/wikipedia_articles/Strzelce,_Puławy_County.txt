Strzelce [ˈstʂɛlt͡sɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nałęczów, within Puławy County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 2 km (1 mi) east of Nałęczów, 24 km (15 mi) south-east of Puławy, and 24 km (15 mi) west of the regional capital Lublin.
