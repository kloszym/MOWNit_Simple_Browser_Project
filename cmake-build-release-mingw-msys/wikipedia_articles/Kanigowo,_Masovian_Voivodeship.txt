Kanigowo [kaniˈɡɔvɔ] is a village located in the administrative district of Gmina Bodzanów, within Płock County, Masovian Voivodeship, situated in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 7 km (4 mi) north-west of Bodzanów, 18 km (11 mi) east of Płock, and 80 km (50 mi) north-west of Warsaw.
