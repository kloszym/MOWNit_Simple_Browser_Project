Golce [ˈɡɔlt͡sɛ] (German: Neugolz) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wałcz, within Wałcz County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 12 kilometres (7 mi) north of Wałcz and 123 km (76 mi) east of the voivodeship capital Szczecin. Golce are inhabited by approximately 300 people.
