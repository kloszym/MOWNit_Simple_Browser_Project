Okhlebinino (Russian: Охлебинино) is a rural locality (a selo) and the administrative centre of Okhlebininsky Selsoviet, Iglinsky District, Bashkortostan, Russia. The population was 924 as of 2010. There are 19 streets.


== Geography ==
Okhlebinino is located 44 km south of Iglino (the district's administrative centre) by road. Muksinovo is the nearest rural locality.
