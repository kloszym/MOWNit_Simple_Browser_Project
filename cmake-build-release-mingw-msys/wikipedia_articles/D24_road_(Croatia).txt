D24 is a state road in the northwestern Croatia connecting Zlatar Bistrica on the D29 state road, near the D14 expressway Zlatar Bistrica interchange, Novi Marof on the D3 state road, the A4 motorway Novi Marof interchange and Ludbreg on the D2 state road. The road is 60.4 km (37.5 mi) long.
Like all other state roads in Croatia, the D24 is managed and maintained by Hrvatske ceste, a state-owned company.


== Traffic volume ==
Traffic is regularly counted and reported by Hrvatske ceste, operator of the road.
