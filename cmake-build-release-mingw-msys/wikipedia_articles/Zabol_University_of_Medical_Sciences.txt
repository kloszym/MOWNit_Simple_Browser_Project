Zabol University of Medical Sciences (Persian: دانشگاه علوم پزشکی و خدمات بهداشتی درمانی زابل, Danushgah-e 'lum-e Pezeshki-ye vâ Xedâmat-e Behedashti-ye Dârmati-ye Zabel) is a public university in Zabol, Iran. The University has five faculties including medicine, pharmacy, health care, nursing, and paramedicine.
