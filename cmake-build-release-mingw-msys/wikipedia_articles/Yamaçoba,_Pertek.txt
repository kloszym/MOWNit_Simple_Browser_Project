Yamaçoba (Kurdish: Yêrindag or Êrindag) is a village in the Pertek District, Tunceli Province, Turkey. The village is populated by Kurds of the Pilvenk tribe and had a population of 41 in 2021.
The hamlets of Havuz (Kurdish: Hewz), Çukurlu (Kurdish: Kort), Taht (Kurdish: Text) and Yayla (Kurdish: Ayle) are attached to the village.
