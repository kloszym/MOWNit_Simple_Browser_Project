Santiphap Channgom (also Chan-ngom, Thai: สันติภาพ จันทร์หง่อม, born 23 September 1996) is a Thai professional footballer who plays as a right-back for Thai League 1 club BG Pathum United and the Thailand national team.


== International career ==
On 12 April 2021, Santiphap was called up for the 2022 World Cup qualification match against Tajikistan. He represented Thailand at the 2023 AFC Asian Cup.
