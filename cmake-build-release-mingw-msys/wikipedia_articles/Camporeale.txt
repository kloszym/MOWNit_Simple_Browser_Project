Camporeale (Sicilian: Campuriali) is a comune (municipality) in the Metropolitan City of Palermo in the Italian region Sicily, located about 35 kilometres (22 mi) southwest of Palermo. As of 2023, it has a population of 2.500 people and an area of 38.6 square kilometres (14.9 sq mi).
Camporeale borders the following municipalities: Alcamo, Monreale.
