Chase Richard Dawson (born June 12, 1997) is an American-Nicaraguan professional baseball player for the Bravos de León of the Mexican League. Having acquired Nicaraguan citizenship in 2025, he represents Nicaragua in international competition.


== Career ==
Dawson played soccer, football, and baseball at Andrean High School, where he won back-to-back Indiana state championships in baseball in 2013 and 2014, before committing to the Valparaiso University for baseball. Over the course of four seasons with the Valparaiso Crusaders from 2016 to 2019, he played 199 games, posting a .276/.364/.382 slash line. He signed with the Gary SouthShore RailCats of the American Association of Professional Baseball on June 11, 2019, and made his professional debut later that day, in a game against the Winnipeg Goldeyes. In 13 games with Gary, he posted a .237/.375/.395 slash line. He was traded to the Schaumburg Boomers of the Frontier League on June 30, 2019.
