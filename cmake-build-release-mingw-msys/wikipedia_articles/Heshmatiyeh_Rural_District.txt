Heshmatiyeh Rural District (Persian: دهستان حشمتیه) is in Eshaqabad District of Zeberkhan County, Razavi Khorasan province, Iran. Its capital is the village of Heshmatiyeh, whose population at the time of the 2016 National Census was 540 in 171 households.


== History ==
After the census, Zeberkhan District was separated from the county in the establishment of Zeberkhan County, and Heshmatiyeh Rural District was created in the new Eshaqabad District.
