Htun Kyi, pen name Minn Latt Yekhaun (Burmese: မင်းလတ်; Rangoon, 19 July 1925-Shan State, c.1985) was a Burmese linguist who studied and published in Czechoslovakia. He returned to Burma to fight as a member of the Burmese Communist Party but was later shot by his own comrades.


== Works ==

Latt, Minn (1958). "The Prague Method Romanization of Burmese". Archiv Orientální. 26: 145–167.
