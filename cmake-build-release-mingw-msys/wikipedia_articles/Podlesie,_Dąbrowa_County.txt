Podlesie [pɔdˈlɛɕɛ] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Szczucin, within Dąbrowa County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) east of Szczucin, 22 km (14 mi) north-east of Dąbrowa Tarnowska, and 93 km (58 mi) east of the regional capital Kraków.
