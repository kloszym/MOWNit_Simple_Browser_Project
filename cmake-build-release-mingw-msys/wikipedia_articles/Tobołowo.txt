Tobołowo [tɔbɔˈwɔvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nowinka, within Augustów County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 14 km (9 mi) north-east of Nowinka, 20 km (12 mi) north-east of Augustów, and 97 km (60 mi) north of the regional capital Białystok.
