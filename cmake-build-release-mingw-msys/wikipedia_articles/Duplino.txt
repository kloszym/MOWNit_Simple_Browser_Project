Duplino (Russian: Дуплино) is a rural locality (a village) in Staroselskoye Rural Settlement, Vologodsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. The population was 5 as of 2002.


== Geography ==
Duplino is located 44 km (27 mi) southwest of Vologda (the district's administrative centre) by road. Andrakovo is the nearest rural locality.
