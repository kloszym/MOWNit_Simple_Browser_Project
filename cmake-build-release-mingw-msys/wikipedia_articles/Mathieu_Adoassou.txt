Eli Mathieu Tchaoussou Adoassou (Arabic: ماثيو أدواسو; born 11 November 1993), known as Mathieu Adoassou, is a Chadian professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Chad Premier League club Renaissance FC and the Chad national team.
