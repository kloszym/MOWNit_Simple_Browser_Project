Büregkhangai (Mongolian: Бүрэгхангай) is a sum (district) of Bulgan Province in northern Mongolia. In 2009, its population is 2,406.


== Geography ==
The district has a total area of 3,497 km2.


== Administrative divisions ==
The district is divided into four bags, which are:

Bumbat
Darkhan
Derst
Tsakht
