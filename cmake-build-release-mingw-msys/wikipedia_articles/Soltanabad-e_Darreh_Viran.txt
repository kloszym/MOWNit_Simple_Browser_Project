Soltanabad-e Darreh Viran (Persian: سلطان آباد دره ويران, also Romanized as Solţānābād-e Darreh Vīrān) is a village in Seylatan Rural District, in the Central District of Bijar County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 122, in 32 families. The village is populated by Azerbaijanis.
