Paulhan (French pronunciation: [polɑ̃], [pojɑ̃]; Occitan pronunciation: [pawˈʎan]) is a commune in Hérault, Occitania, Southern France.
Its inhabitants are called Paulhanais (male) or Paulhanaises (female).
