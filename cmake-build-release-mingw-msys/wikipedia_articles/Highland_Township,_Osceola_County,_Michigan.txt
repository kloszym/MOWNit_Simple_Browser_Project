Highland Township is a civil township of Osceola County in the U.S. state of Michigan. The population was 1,148 at the 2020 census.


== Communities ==
There are no incorporated municipalities or large population centers in the township. There are some named unincorporated communities or locales in the township:

Highland,  centered on the junction of 23 Mile Road and 90th Avenue at 44°08′44″N 85°15′59″W
Ina,  centered on the junction of 21 Mile Road and 100th Avenue at 44°07′01″N 85°17′12″W A post office operated from March 1899 until December 1907.
Pisgah Heights, on 70th Avenue between 19 Mile and 20 Mile roads at 44°05′40″N 85°13′34″W near an old railroad grade.
The village of Le Roy is to the southwest. The post office (named "Leroy") with ZIP code, 49655, also serves the southwest corner of the township.
The village of Marion is to the east and the Marion ZIP code, 49665, serves the eastern half of the township.
The village of Tustin is to the west and the Tustin ZIP code, 49688, serves the western portion of the township.
