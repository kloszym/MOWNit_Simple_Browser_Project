Chruścin [ˈxruɕt͡ɕin] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Dąbie, within Koło County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) west of Dąbie, 16 km (10 mi) south-east of Koło, and 131 km (81 mi) east of the regional capital Poznań.
