John-Michael Shenette (born May 20, 1986) is an American professional stock car racing driver and team owner who competes part-time in the NASCAR Whelen Modified Tour, driving and fielding the No. 8 for his own team.
Shenette has previously competed in series such as the SMART Modified Tour, the PASS National Championship Super Late Model Series, the PASS South Super Late Model Series, and the Granite State Pro Stock Series.
