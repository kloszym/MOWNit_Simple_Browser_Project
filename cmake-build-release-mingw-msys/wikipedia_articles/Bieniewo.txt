Bieniewo [bjɛˈɲɛvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Lubomino, within Lidzbark County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 19 kilometres (12 mi) west of Lidzbark Warmiński and 38 km (24 mi) north of the regional capital Olsztyn.
