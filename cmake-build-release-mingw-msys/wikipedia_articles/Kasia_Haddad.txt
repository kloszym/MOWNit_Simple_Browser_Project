Kasia Haddad (born 1976 or 1977) is a British actress. She portrayed the role of Josie Jump for series 3 and 4 in the children's television programme Balamory (replacing Buki Akib) and Wendy Parnell in an episode of Doctors.
Haddad also starred in the Balamory DVD special Seeking Santa.
