Reza Dehdar  (Persian: رضا دهدار, born 2 July 1995 in Ramhormoz) is an Iranian weightlifter. He won the silver medal in the men's 102 kg event at the 2022 World Weightlifting Championships held in Bogotá, Colombia. He won a bronze medal at the 2019 World Weightlifting Championships.
