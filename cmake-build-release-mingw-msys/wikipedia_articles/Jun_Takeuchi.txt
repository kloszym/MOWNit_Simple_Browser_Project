Jun Takeuchi (竹内 潤, Takeuchi Jun) (born August 11, 1970) is a Japanese video game director, designer and producer.
Takeuchi graduated from the Division of Graphics at the Sozosha College of Design and joined the game development company Capcom in 1991. He produced Onimusha 3 and Lost Planet and chiefly produced Resident Evil 5. He worked on other Capcom games such as Street Fighter II for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System and Resident Evil and Resident Evil 2 for the PlayStation.
In 2012, Takeuchi became head of Capcom's Development Division 1, which developed Resident Evil, Devil May Cry and other titles for the Western world.
