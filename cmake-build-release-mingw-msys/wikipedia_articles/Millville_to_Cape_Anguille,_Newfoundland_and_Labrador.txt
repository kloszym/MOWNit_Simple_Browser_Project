Millville to Cape Anguille is a designated place in the Canadian province of Newfoundland and Labrador.


== Geography ==
Millville to Cape Anguille is in Newfoundland within Subdivision A of Division No. 4.


== Demographics ==
As a designated place in the 2016 Census of Population conducted by Statistics Canada, Millville to Cape Anguille recorded a population of 363 living in 159 of its 167 total private dwellings, a change of -23.6% from its 2011 population of 475. With a land area of 17.15 km2 (6.62 sq mi), it had a population density of 21.2/km2 (54.8/sq mi) in 2016.
