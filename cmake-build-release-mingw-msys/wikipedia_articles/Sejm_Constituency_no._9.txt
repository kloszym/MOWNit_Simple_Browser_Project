Sejm Constituency no. 9 (Polish: Okręg wyborczy nr 9 do Sejmu) is a Polish constituency represented in the Sejm, lower house of Polish parliament, by ten members. It is located in the Łódź Voivodeship and comprises the city of Łódź, Brzeziny and Łódź East counties.
Corresponding constituencies for the Senate are numbered 23 and 24.
