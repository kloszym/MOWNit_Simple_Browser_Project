Itokawa (written: 糸川 lit. "thread river") is a Japanese surname. Notable people with the surname include:

Hideo Itokawa (糸川 英夫, 1912–1999), Japanese rocket scientist
Masaaki Itokawa (糸川 正晃, born 1974), Japanese politician of the People's New Party
Teruo Itokawa (糸川 照雄, 1940–2000), Japanese shot putter
Toshihiko Itokawa (糸川 敏彦, born 1974), Japanese speed skater
