Roździn [ˈrɔʑd͡ʑin] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kluki, within Bełchatów County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north of Kluki, 10 km (6 mi) west of Bełchatów, and 46 km (29 mi) south of the regional capital Łódź.
