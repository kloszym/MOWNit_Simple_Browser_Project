Thung Mamao railway station is a railway station located in Ao Noi Subdistrict, Prachuap Khiri Khan City, Prachuap Khiri Khan. It is a class 3 railway station located 289.034 km (179.6 mi) from Thon Buri railway station.


== Train services ==
Ordinary 251/252 Bang Sue Junction-Prachuap Khiri Khan-Bang Sue Junction
Ordinary 254/255 Lang Suan-Thon Buri-Lang Suan
