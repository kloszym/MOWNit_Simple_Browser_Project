Lenny Druyts (born 18 May 1997) is a Belgian professional racing cyclist, who currently rides for UCI Women's Continental Team Chevalmeire. As a junior rider she competed in the women's junior road race at the 2014 UCI Road World Championships and 2015 UCI Road World Championships.
Her sisters Jessy Druyts, Demmy Druyts and Kelly Druyts, as well as her brother Gerry Druyts are all professional cyclists.
