Uwade Akhere, known mononymously as Uwade, is a Nigerian-American indie folk musician from Charlotte, North Carolina.


== History ==
Uwade attended Columbia University, graduating in 2021. Uwade released their first release the same year, titled The Man Who Sees Tomorrow/Lodarore. In 2022, Uwade released a new song titled "Do You See The Light Around Me?" In 2025, Uwade released their debut album, Florilegium, on Thirty Tigers. Uwade cited Fleet Foxes, Kate Bush, and Sister Rosetta Tharpe as influences for the album.
