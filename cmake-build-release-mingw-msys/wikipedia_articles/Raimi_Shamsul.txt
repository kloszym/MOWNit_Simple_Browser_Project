Muhammad Raimi bin Shamsul (born 28 October 2002) is a Malaysian professional footballer who plays as a right back for Malaysia Super League club Selangor.


== Club career ==


=== Selangor ===
On early career, Raimi signed for Selangor from Academy Mokhtar Dahari at the age of 19. He then progressed through the club's youth setup, playing for the under-21 and under-23 squads. == International career ==


=== Youth ===
On 30 October 2019, Raimi was called up to the Malaysia under-19 by Brad Maloney for the 2020 AFC U-19 Championship qualification in Cambodia.
