Kabetsovo (Russian: Кабецово) is a rural locality (a village) in Kemskoye Rural Settlement, Vytegorsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. As of 2002 its population was 8 people.


== Geography ==
Kabetsovo is located 84 km southeast of Vytegra (the district's administrative centre) by road. Mirny is the nearest locality. Lake Indoman is the nearest lake to Kabetsovo.
