Boris Nicola Bakalov (Bulgarian: Борис Никола Бакалов, born 23 November 1980) is a former professional Bulgarian tennis player. On 21 November 2011, he reached his highest ATP singles ranking of 621 whilst his best doubles ranking was 457 on 4 July 2011. He played for and graduated from Azusa Pacific University in 2006.
