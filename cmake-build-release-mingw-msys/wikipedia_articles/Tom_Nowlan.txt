Tom Nowlan is an Australian rugby union player who plays for the Rangers Vicenza in Italian Serie A Elite. His playing position is lock.
In Spring 2021, he was named in the Rebels squad for Round 3 of the 2021 Super Rugby AU season. He played for Rebels in Super Rugby also in 2022 Super Rugby season He previously represented NSW Country Eagles in the 2019 National Rugby Championship.
