Novoromanovka (Russian: Новоромановка) is a rural locality (a selo) in Tarumovsky District, Republic of Dagestan, Russia. Population: 1,322 (2021 Census);  1,132 (2002 Census); 863 (1989 Soviet census). There are 10 streets.


== Geography ==
Novoromanovka is located 24 km southeast of Tarumovka (the district's administrative centre) by road. Novogeorgiyevka is the nearest rural locality.
