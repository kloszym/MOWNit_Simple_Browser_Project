South Andros Airport (IATA: TZN, ICAO: MYAK) is an airport near Congo Town. In South Andros, part of Andros Island is in The Bahamas. It is also known as Congo Town Airport (IATA: COX).


== Facilities ==
The airport resides at an elevation of 15 ft (4.6 m) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 10/28 with an asphalt surface measuring 1,623 m × 30 m (5,325 ft × 98 ft).
