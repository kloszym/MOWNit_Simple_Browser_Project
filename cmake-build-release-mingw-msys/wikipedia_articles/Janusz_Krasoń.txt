Janusz Mirosław Krasoń (pronounced [ˈjanuʂ ˈkrasɔɲ]; born 15 September 1956 in Jawor) is a Polish politician. He was elected to the Sejm on 25 September 2005, getting 15,162 votes in 3 Wrocław district as a candidate from the Democratic Left Alliance list.
He was also a member of Sejm 2001-2005.
