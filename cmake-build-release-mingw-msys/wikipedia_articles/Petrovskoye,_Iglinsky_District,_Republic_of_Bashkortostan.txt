Petrovskoye (Russian: Петровское) is a rural locality (a village) in Kaltovsky Selsoviet, Iglinsky District, Bashkortostan, Russia. The population was 15 as of 2010. There are 3 streets in the district.


== Geography ==
Petrovskoye is located 34 km southeast of Iglino (the district's administrative centre) by road. Kommunar is the nearest rural locality.
