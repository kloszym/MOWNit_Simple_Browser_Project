Hsieh Chang-heng (Chinese: 謝長亨; pinyin: Yáng Jiànfú; Wade–Giles: Hsieh4 Chang2 Heng1; born 22 January 1962) is a pitcher in the CPBL. He played for Uni-President Lions. He is the 1st person achieved the record of career with 100 wins. After retired, he became the manager of the Lions and then Chinatrust Whales until it went defunct in 2008.
