Chrzanów Mały [ˈxʂanuf ˈmawɨ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Grodzisk Mazowiecki, within Grodzisk Mazowiecki County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 km (3 mi) north of Grodzisk Mazowiecki and 27 km (17 mi) west of Warsaw.
