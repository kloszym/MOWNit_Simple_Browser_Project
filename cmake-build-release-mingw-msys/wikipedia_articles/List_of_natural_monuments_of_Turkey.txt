This is a list of natural monuments of Turkey. As of July 1, 2015, there are 112 natural monuments (Turkish: Tabiat anıtı) comprising caves, landforms, waterfalls and mostly old trees (Tree). These protected natural or cultural features of outstanding or unique value are administered by the Directorate-General of Nature Protection and National Parks (Turkish: Doğa Koruma ve Milli Parklar Genel Müdürlüğü) of the Ministry of Environment and Forest.
