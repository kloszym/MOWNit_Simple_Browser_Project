Jesse Marschke ( MARSH-key,) (born 19 June 1997) is an Australian professional rugby league player who played as a halfback and five-eighth for the St. George Illawarra Dragons in the National Rugby League (NRL). He is the brother of former Sydney Roosters player Ben Marschke.
