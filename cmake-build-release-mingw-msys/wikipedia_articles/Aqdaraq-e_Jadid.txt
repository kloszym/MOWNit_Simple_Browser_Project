Aqdaraq-e Jadid (Persian: اق درق جديد, also Romanized as Āqdaraq-e Jadīd; also known as Āgh Daraq-e Jadīd) is a village in Peyghan Chayi Rural District, in the Central District of Kaleybar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 235, in 44 families.
