Wola Aleksandra [ˈvɔla alɛkˈsandra] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nieporęt, within Legionowo County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 km (3 mi) south-west of Nieporęt, 5 km (3 mi) east of Legionowo, and 21 km (13 mi) north of Warsaw.
