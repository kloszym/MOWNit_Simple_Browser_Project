Sphaerius martini is an extinct species of beetle in the family Sphaeriusidae that lived during the Cenomanian stage of the Late Cretaceous epoch. The holotype measures about 0.6 mm (0.024 in) in length and 0.44 mm (0.017 in) in width.


== Distribution ==
Sphaerius martini is known from fossilised specimens found in Burmese amber.
