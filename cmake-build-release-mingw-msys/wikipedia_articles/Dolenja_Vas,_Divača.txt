Dolenja Vas (pronounced [dɔˈleːnja ˈʋaːs]; Slovene: Dolenja vas, Italian: Villabassa (di Senosècchia)) is a village in the Municipality of Divača in the Littoral region of Slovenia.


== Church ==

The local church is dedicated to Our Lady of Sorrows and belongs to the Parish of Senožeče.
