== Early life ==
She was born on February 8, 1900, at Jagadalpur in erstwhile princely state of Bastar. Her father Daniel Sabat was a physician. Her mother's name was Monika Sabat. Her maternal grandfather was from Dandamukundapur, Puri.  Her father moved to Bastar and converted to Christianity before her birth. Soon after her birth she moved to Burma with her family. She spent her early childhood in Burma. Her father remarried while at Burma and Kuntala returned to Odisha with her Mother. She settled in Khordha with her mother after returning from Burma. In spite of total lack of women's education her mother's perseverance allowed her to receive a good education.
