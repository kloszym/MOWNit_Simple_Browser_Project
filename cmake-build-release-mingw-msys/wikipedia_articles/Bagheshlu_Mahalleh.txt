Bagheshlu Mahalleh (Persian: باغشلومحله, also Romanized as Bāgheshlū Maḩalleh; also known as Bāghemsheh Maḩalleh and Bāghesh Maḩalleh-ye Ḩavīq) is a village in Haviq Rural District, Haviq District, Talesh County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 523, in 110 families.
