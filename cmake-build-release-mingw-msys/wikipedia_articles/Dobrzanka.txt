Dobrzanka [dɔˈbʐanka] is a former village in the administrative district of Gmina Bircza, within Przemyśl County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south-west of Bircza, 30 km (19 mi) south-west of Przemyśl, and 50 km (31 mi) south-east of the regional capital Rzeszów.
