Touat-Cheikh Sidi Mohamed Belkebir Airport (IATA: AZR, ICAO: DAUA) is a public airport located 6 nm (11 km) southeast of Adrar, the capital of the Adrar province (wilaya) in Algeria.


== Facilities ==
The airport resides at an elevation of 280 metres (919 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 04/22 with an asphalt surface measuring 3,200 by 60 metres (10,499 ft × 197 ft).
