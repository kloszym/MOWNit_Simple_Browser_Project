Klewinowo [klɛviˈnɔvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Juchnowiec Kościelny, within Białystok County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 6 km (4 mi) south-east of Juchnowiec Kościelny and 15 km (9 mi) south of the regional capital Białystok.
