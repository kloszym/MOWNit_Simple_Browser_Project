Akram Bouras (Arabic: أكرم بوراس; born February 23, 2002) is an Algerian professional footballer who plays for Algerian Ligue Professionnelle 1 club MC Alger.
In August 2024, Bouras signed a three-year contract with Mouloudia Algiers.


== Honours ==
Algerian Ligue Professionnelle 1: 2020–21, 2021–22, 2022–23
Algerian Cup: 2023–24
