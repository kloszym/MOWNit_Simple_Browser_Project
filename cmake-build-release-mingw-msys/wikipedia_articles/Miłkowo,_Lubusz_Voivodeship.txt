Miłkowo [miu̯ˈkɔvɔ] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Świebodzin, within Świebodzin County, Lubusz Voivodeship, in western Poland. It lies approximately 11 km (7 mi) south-east of Świebodzin, 27 km (17 mi) north of Zielona Góra, and 68 km (42 mi) south of Gorzów Wielkopolski.
