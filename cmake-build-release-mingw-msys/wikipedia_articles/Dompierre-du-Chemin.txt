Dompierre-du-Chemin (French pronunciation: [dɔ̃pjɛʁ dy ʃəmɛ̃]; Breton: Dompêr-an-Hent; Gallo: Donpièrr) is a former commune in the Ille-et-Vilaine department in Brittany in northwestern France. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune Luitré-Dompierre.


== Population ==
Inhabitants of Dompierre-du-Chemin are called Dompierrais in French.
