Goluzino (Russian: Голузино) is a rural locality (a village) in Kichmegnskoye Rural Settlement, Kichmengsko-Gorodetsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. The population was 72 as of 2002.


== Geography ==
Goluzino is located 19 km southwest of Kichmengsky Gorodok (the district's administrative centre) by road. Gridenskaya is the nearest rural locality.
