Iasonas Stavropoulos (Greek: Ιάσονας Σταυρόπουλος; born 10 January 2000) is a Greek professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Super League 2 club Asteras Vlachioti.


== Career ==


=== Panachaiki ===
On 16 August 2018, Stavropoulos signed a professional contract with Panachaiki.
On 1 March 2020, Stavropoulos made his professional debut against Chania.
