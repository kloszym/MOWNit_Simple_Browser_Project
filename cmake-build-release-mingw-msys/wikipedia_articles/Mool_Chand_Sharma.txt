Mool Chand Sharma (born 4 April 1964) is Cabinet Minister of Transport in the Government of Haryana. Apart from it, he has 3 other portfolios of Mines & Geology, Higher Education and Elections.
Sharma is the member of the Haryana Legislative Assembly from the BJP representing the Ballabhgarh Vidhan sabha Constituency in Haryana since 2014. He has done many other good works in taking Ballabhgarh city forward.
