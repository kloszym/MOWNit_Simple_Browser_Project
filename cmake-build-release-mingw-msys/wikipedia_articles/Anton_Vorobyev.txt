Anton Gennadyevich Vorobyev (Russian: Антон Геннадьевич Воробьёв; born 12 October 1990) is a Russian cyclist, who last rode for UCI Professional Continental team Gazprom–RusVelo. In 2012, he became the World Under-23 time trial champion on a hilly course.
