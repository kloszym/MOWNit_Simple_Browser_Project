Ludwikowo [ludviˈkɔvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wągrowiec, within Wągrowiec County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 11 kilometres (7 mi) north-east of Wągrowiec and 57 km (35 mi) north-east of the regional capital Poznań.
