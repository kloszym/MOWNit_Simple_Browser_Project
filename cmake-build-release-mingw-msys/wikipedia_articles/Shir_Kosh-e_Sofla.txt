Shir Kosh-e Sofla (Persian: شيركش سفلي, also Romanized as Shīr Kosh-e Soflá; also known as Sarkosh-e Pā’īn, Sarkūsh-e Pā’īn, and Sarkush Pāin) is a village in Najafabad Rural District, in the Central District of Bijar County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 173, in 31 families. The village is populated by Kurds.
