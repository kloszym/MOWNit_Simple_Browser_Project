Acorán Juan Hernández Mendoza (born (1990-12-30)30 December 1990 in Tenerife) is a Spanish male weightlifter, competing in the 62 kg category and representing Spain at international competitions. He competed at world championships, most recently at the 2011 World Weightlifting Championships.
