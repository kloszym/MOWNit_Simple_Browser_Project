Jackson Township is a township in Sharp County, Arkansas, United States. Its total population was 295 as of the 2010 United States Census, a decrease of 9.51 percent from 326 at the 2000 census.

According to the 2010 Census, Jackson Township is located at 36°13′32″N 91°24′48″W (36.225564, -91.413279). It has a total area of 26.923 square miles (69.73 square kilometers); of which 26.724 sq mi (69.21 km2) is land and 0.199 sq mi (0.52 km2) is water (0.74%). As per the USGS National Elevation Dataset, the elevation is 364 feet (111 m).
The town of Williford is located within the township.
