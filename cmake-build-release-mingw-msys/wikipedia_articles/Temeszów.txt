Temeszów [tɛˈmɛʂuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Dydnia, within Brzozów County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) east of Dydnia, 15 km (9 mi) east of Brzozów, and 41 km (25 mi) south of the regional capital Rzeszów.
