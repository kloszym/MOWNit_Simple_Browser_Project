Yurino (Russian: Юрино) is a rural locality (a village) in Klyazminskoye Rural Settlement, Kovrovsky District, Vladimir Oblast, Russia. The population was 82 as of 2010.


== Geography ==
Yurino is located on the Klyazma River, 36 km northeast of Kovrov (the district's administrative centre) by road. Doronikha is the nearest rural locality.
