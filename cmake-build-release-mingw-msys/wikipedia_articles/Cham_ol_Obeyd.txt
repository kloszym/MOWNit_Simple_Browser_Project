Cham ol Obeyd (Persian: چم العبيد, also Romanized as Cham ol 'Obeyd and Chamm ol 'Obeyd; also known as Chammolābād and Ḩamlābād) is a village in Anaqcheh Rural District, in the Central District of Ahvaz County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 585, in 105 families.
